Faraz Nahyan

Toronto · Engineer · Entrepreneur

Faraz Nahyan is an electrical engineer and entrepreneur based in Toronto, working at the intersection of biomedical technology and human experience. Outside of building, he shoots portrait photography and performs classical and jazz piano.

Currently

  • Developing a retinal prosthetics venture through Brilliant Catalyst, Ontario Tech
  • Completing a B.Eng. in Electrical Engineering — prior B.Sc. in Biochemistry
  • Building at the intersection of biological and inorganic interfaces
  • Open to engineering roles, research collaborations, and photography commissions

Selected work

Biomedical · Venture

Retinal Prosthetics

Developing a retinal prosthetic device through the Brilliant Catalyst incubator at Ontario Tech. Focused on biological-to-inorganic interfacing — translating electrical signals across the boundary of living tissue and synthetic substrate to restore functional vision.
